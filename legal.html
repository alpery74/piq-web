Subscription Terms & Billing

PIQ Labs offers auto-renewable subscriptions that unlock higher usage limits and advanced analytics. Current plan options are:

  • Free Plan: No charge. Includes up to 5 holdings per analysis, 5 analyses per month, and our agentic AI portfolio lab for factor diagnostics, diversification math, Monte Carlo stress testing, optimization playbooks, and tax-efficiency considerations.
  • Basic Plan: $2.99 per month or $29.99 per year. Includes up to 20 holdings per analysis, 10 analyses per month, and all Free plan capabilities.
  • Plus Plan: $5.99 per month or $59.99 per year. Includes up to 40 holdings per analysis, 20 analyses per month, and all Basic plan capabilities.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the current period ends. Payment is charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase and on renewal. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. Restoring purchases is available within the app for users who reinstall or switch devices.
